{# ── Step-by-step overview ── #}
  • 1
    Risk Preference Elicitation

    You will choose between five investment options, each with two equally likely payoffs. This is a one-time decision that cannot be changed once submitted. Your choice directly impacts your final payout — one option will be randomly selected and paid out at the end of the study.

    Your choice will also be used to calibrate the personalized portfolios that the Human Wealth Manager and AI Advisor would construct on your behalf in Stage 3.

  • 2
    Portfolio Construction

    You will allocate 100% of a hypothetical investment account across five asset classes — T-Bills, Index Funds, REITs, Common Stocks, and Cryptocurrency. This portfolio will serve as your personal baseline in Stage 3.

    At some point during the study you will also be asked to indicate your beliefs about how a Human Wealth Manager and an AI Advisor are each likely to perform, using a token-allocation task.

  • 3
    Advisor Valuation

    You will complete two separate valuation tasks — one for a Human Wealth Manager and one for an AI Advisor. For each, you will see a table with 10 rows presenting the same choice at increasing advisory fees ($1 through $10):

    • Keep your portfolio — You retain the portfolio you built in Stage 2. It contributes to your participation payout based on its simulated performance.
    • Replace your portfolio — Your portfolio is replaced by the advisor's portfolio, which is built using the risk preferences you revealed in Stage 1. You pay the listed fee out of your participation earnings, and the advisor's portfolio determines your payout instead.

    Use the arrow buttons (← / →) in each row to record your preference at that fee level. Selecting an option will automatically update all other rows to keep your choices consistent. There are no right or wrong answers — please respond based on your genuine preferences.
