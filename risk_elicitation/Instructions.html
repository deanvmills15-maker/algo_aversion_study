{# ── Step-by-step overview ── #}
  • 1
    Risk Preference Elicitation

    You will choose between five investment options, each with two equally likely payoffs. This is a one-time decision that cannot be changed once submitted. Your choice directly impacts your final payout — one option will be randomly selected and paid out at the end of the study.

    Your choice will also be used to calibrate a personalized investment portfolio that will be presented to you later in the study.

  • 2
    Portfolio Construction & Beliefs

    You will build a portfolio across five asset classes and indicate your beliefs about how each type of investment advisor is likely to perform.

  • 3
    Advisor Valuation

    Across five rounds, you will state the maximum fee you would pay to have either a Human Wealth Manager or an AI Advisor manage your portfolio. One decision will be randomly selected to determine your bonus payment.
